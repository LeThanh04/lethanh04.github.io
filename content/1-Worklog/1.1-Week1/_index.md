### Week 1 Achievements:

* Complete the AWS account registration process, familiarize yourself with the AWS Management Console interface, and understand how to access and utilize basic services on the AWS platform.

* Complete five foundational practical exercises outlined in the training curriculum to become accustomed to the learning workflow, the use of instructional materials, and the methodology for executing AWS labs.

* Study and explore the functions of key AWS services—such as Amazon EC2, Amazon S3, Amazon VPC, and AWS IAM—to gain a comprehensive overview of the AWS service ecosystem.

* Proactively research additional resources via AWS Documentation, AWS Skill Builder, and company-provided video tutorials to reinforce your knowledge, while documenting key concepts for future review and practice.
