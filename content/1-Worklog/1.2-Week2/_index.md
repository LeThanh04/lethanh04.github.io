### Week 2 Achievements:

* Explore AWS Identity and Access Management (IAM) and understand the functions of:
 * Root User
 * IAM User
 * IAM Group
 * IAM Policy

* Gain hands-on experience creating IAM Users and IAM Groups, assigning access permissions via existing IAM Policies, and understanding the principle of Least Privilege.

* Learn to use AWS Budgets to monitor costs, set up budget alerts, and prevent unexpected expenses during practice sessions.

* Acquire foundational knowledge of Amazon VPC and understand the roles of VPCs, Subnets, Internet Gateways, and Route Tables in building network infrastructure on AWS.

* Grasp the concepts and operating principles of VPNs, and understand the role of VPNs in securely connecting internal systems with the cloud environment.
