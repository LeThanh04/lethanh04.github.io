During my participation in the **Workforce Bootcamp – First Cloud AI Journey (FCAJ)** program from **April 17, 2026** to **July 10, 2026**, I had the opportunity to learn and gain hands-on experience with the **Amazon Web Services (AWS)** cloud platform while becoming familiar with the end-to-end development process of a cloud-based application.

Throughout the internship program, I studied and practiced using a variety of essential AWS services, including **Amazon EC2, Amazon S3, Amazon RDS, Amazon DynamoDB, AWS Lambda, Amazon API Gateway, Amazon CloudWatch, and AWS Identity and Access Management (IAM)**. In addition to completing weekly hands-on labs, I participated in workshops, technical activities, and collaborated closely with my team to develop the **AWS BILLO** project based on a **Serverless architecture** on AWS.
