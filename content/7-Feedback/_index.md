> Here, you can freely share your personal opinions about your experience participating in the First Cloud AI Journey program. This will help the FCAJ team improve any shortcomings based on the following aspects:

### Overall Evaluation

**1. Working Environment**  
Throughout my internship, I experienced a professional and friendly work environment that actively encouraged a spirit of learning. Mentors and fellow program members were always ready to listen, answer questions, and provide support whenever I faced challenges in my learning or project development. This helped me gain confidence in approaching new technologies and successfully completing my assigned tasks.

**2. Support from Mentor / Team Admin**  
The team of mentors closely monitors each group's progress and regularly provides constructive feedback. In addition to guiding the use of AWS services—such as EC2, Lambda, API Gateway, DynamoDB, and S3—mentors share their expertise on system architecture design, solution optimization, and troubleshooting issues that arise during implementation. The administrative team also provides prompt support regarding documentation, office schedules, and program activities.

**3. Relevance of Work to Academic Major**  
The internship content is closely aligned with my Information Technology major. The program provided me with the opportunity to apply the knowledge I had acquired to build a real-world project on the AWS platform. Through this experience, I gained a deeper understanding of the cloud application development process and how to implement serverless solutions in a practical environment.

**4. Learning & Skill Development Opportunities**  
The program offered numerous opportunities for me to enhance both my technical knowledge and professional skills. I gained hands-on experience with various AWS services while also improving my abilities in researching documentation, teamwork, time management, and presenting project results. Independently exploring official AWS documentation also helped me cultivate a habit of proactive learning and develop problem-solving skills.

**5. Company Culture & Team Spirit**  
The spirit of cooperation among members is a highlight of the program. Everyone is always ready to share experiences, support one another during project development, and collaborate on solutions when facing challenges. As a result, I have not only gained a wealth of new knowledge but also honed my communication and teamwork skills.

**6. Internship Policies / Benefits**  
The program features a clear training plan, enabling interns to easily track the objectives of each stage. Interns also participate in workshops, meetups, and knowledge-sharing sessions, providing opportunities to expand their expertise and connect with like-minded individuals in the cloud computing field.
